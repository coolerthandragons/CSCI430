About Rachel Cubbage

Rachel Cubbage grew up in a small town in Stanley, Virginia. From a young age, she always had a book in her hand and enjoyed reading as much as she could. As she grew older, she began being interested in writing fantasy, horror, and general fiction since all of these genres were what she enjoyed reading. She has been to Paris, France, London, England, and hopes to travel more around the world.
